:fire_extinguisher:Blue Teaming :large_blue_diamond:

Modern cybersecurity, we believe, necessitates a distinct attitude and operational style, particularly for digital organizations with cloud-native operations, such that: Assume compromise, but don't reveal any particular compromise point. You can't safeguard what you can't see, so keep track of everything. The four fundamental activities that comprise these cyber security concepts are as follows; Govern: Recognizing and addressing security threats. Reduce security risks by implementing security controls. Cybersecurity Awareness Training, Cybersecurity Audit, Vulnerability Scanning, and Penetration Testing are the foundations of cybersecurity.

:fire:Red & Purple Teaming :red_circle: :purple_square:

When in doubt, Red Team it. The goal of a Red Team is to take on the role of adversary, or worst-case scenario. It's all about paying attention, not following the rules, and adapting...I take advantage of the fact that there are an infinite number of security vulnerabilities for a given system, program, or strategy, the majority of which will never be discovered, and I combine the blue and red teams into a single team to deliver value to the business. A successful purple team brings together two sets of people who would ordinarily work on opposite sides of the table to work together on a common goal: increasing cybersecurity. This field necessitates creativity, skepticism, and a peculiar sense of humor, all of which are aces up my sleeve!
